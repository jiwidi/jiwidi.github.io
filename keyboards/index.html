//jaime/keyboards:_

Many keyboards!

I spend a lot of time at a keyboard. When I'm using the computer, the keyboard is essentially an extension of myself. The majority of ordinary keyboards are good and functional, however if you want to improve your typing, you can go *pretty overkill*. These overkilled keyboards perform the same tasks as a standard keyboard and don't offer any unique functionality, but they feel comfortable to type on and are often easier to use for extended periods of time without experiencing hand strain. The soldering, picking out the components, and assembling it all just brings out the engineer in me.
