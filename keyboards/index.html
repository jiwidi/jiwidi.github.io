//jaime/keyboards:_

Many keyboards!

As a computer guy I spend tons of time attached to a keyboard. The keyboard is basically an extension of me while working on the computer. Most common keyboards are nice and do the job, but you can get *pretty overkill* if you want to perfectionize your typing experience. These overkilled keyboards dont have any special functionality and do the same as a plain old keyboard, but they just feel nice to type on and often easier to use for longer periods of time without fatigue on your hands The soldering, choosing different parts and putting it all together just brings the engineer inside of me.
