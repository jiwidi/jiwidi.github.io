Photography

In our fast-moving, digital age, the power of photography to make us pause and reflect amazes me. It gives us a tangible, physical way to remember how fleeting moments can be. There's something incredibly beautiful about how quickly life's moments flash by.

Photography stands out to me as a refreshing break from the ordered, logical aspects of my daily life and work. It's my escape into a realm not bound by rules.
